✦ Signature Treatments
Thai Massage
50 min 300 ₪

Rooted in over 2,500 years of healing tradition, Thai Massage is a meditative, floor-based practice that merges acupressure, assisted yoga stretching, and energy line work. Performed fully clothed on a comfortable floor mat, this treatment uses Liora's hands, forearms, elbows, and body weight to apply rhythmic, targeted pressure along the body's energy meridians.

Unlike table massages, Thai Massage is interactive: Liora will guide your body through a flowing sequence of passive stretches and compressions, improving flexibility, joint mobility, and the free flow of vital energy (prana). Clients often describe the experience as "being folded into peace". Particularly recommended for those who feel stiff, blocked, or mentally overwhelmed.

Hot Stone Massage
60 min 300 ₪

The Hot Stone Massage is a deeply therapeutic ritual that harnesses the ancient healing power of volcanic basalt stones, heated to the perfect temperature. The warmth penetrates far deeper than hands alone could reach — relaxing muscle fibers at their core, dissolving knots that have resisted ordinary massage, and opening energy channels throughout the body.

Smooth stones are placed along the spine, in the palms, and between the toes — key energetic points — while Liora glides heated stones along the muscles with gentle, intentional strokes. The sensation is one of profound warmth, surrender, and timelessness. This treatment is especially beneficial for chronic tension, poor circulation, anxiety, and insomnia. An experience you will carry in your body long after you leave.
